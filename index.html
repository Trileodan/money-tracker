Settings

Budget targets (50 / 30 / 20)

Targets are a % of your income, suggested using the classic Needs 50% · Wants 30% · Savings 20% rule. Edit any value — the dashboard flags categories you're over on.

Your regular income

Set your normal take-home pay and how often you're paid. Category percentages are always measured against this income, scaled to the timeframe you're viewing — so a "no-payday" week no longer looks terrible. The Income tile still shows what you actually received.

Install on your phone

iPhone: open this file in Safari, tap the Share button, then "Add to Home Screen".

Android: open in Chrome, tap the ⋮ menu, then "Add to Home screen".

It then opens full-screen like a normal app and works offline.
